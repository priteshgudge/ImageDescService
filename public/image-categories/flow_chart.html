Example (flowchart as an illustration of a process): The figure is titled “The Krebs Cycle,” and shows a linear reaction that leads to a cyclical reaction. The steps of each reaction are shown as lists:




  1. Linear Reaction
    1. Step 1
    2. Step 2
    3. Step 3
    4. Step 4 - the enzyme leaves the reaction and delivers the acetyl group to the circular reaction
  2. Circular Reaction
    1. Step 1
    2. Step 2
    3. Step 3
    4. re-enters Step 1 with the acetyl group that enters from the linear reaction

Guidelines:
  • Provide the title
  • Provide a brief overview of how the chart is organized, including if it depicts multiple starting points, or a diagram of a linear or circular process
  • Organize the information as a nested list, or outline
  • If there are multiple paths in the flow chart, divide each pathway into a nested list
