Example:This illustration is titled “Carbon Cycle.” Farms, forests, rivers, oceans, and industry are shown. Four arrows pointing from one to another form a circle around the diagram. Smaller arrows within the circle illustrate the following processes: Carbon Storage, and Fluxes in Carbon. The Carbon of each process are shown in the following tables.

Guidelines:

  • Provide the title of the diagram
  • Provide a brief overview of the scene, then describe details according to the flow of how it is presented in the diagram
  • When possible, present the data as a table with row or column headings, or as a nested list
